Born and raised in Peru, Chef Iole loved cooking for her family. After graduating college she attended Cordon Bleu to perfect her craft.

At Panca, all desserts, sauces and marinades are homemade with only the freshest, local quality ingrediants. Our Hormone free chicken is marinated for up to two days in a homemade marinade with special spices including the tasty, mild Panca pepper, before slow cooking on the rotisserie.

Chef Iole's dream to share the savory flavors, as well as the culture and history of Peru with others on a larger scale materialized when PANCA opened in Oceanside, CA, on February 6, 2012.
